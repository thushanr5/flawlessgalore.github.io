Batik is a wax dyeing technique used on cloth clothing. Many batik clothes have unique patterns such as stripes, squares, dots, and more. Batik is a prominent type of clothing in various East Asian countries such as Indonesia and South Asian such as India and Sri Lanka. Batik is found in many markets in such countries and is available to buy online and is shipped from countries that manufacture them.

Flawless Galore is a buisness founded by Chandrika Ganewatte and her friends. It sells clothes locally and plans on expanding to go online so that people online can purchase and enjoy Batik clothing.

Flawless Galore is currently expanding and will be growing slowly. It was inspired by Sri Lankan clothes. This website will allow payments in the future as per the current plans.
